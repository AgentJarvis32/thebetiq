    New York Gambling Taxes

    New York has one of the most aggressive tax structures in the country — both for operators and players.

    For players:

  • **Federal:** All gambling winnings taxable as ordinary income
  • **New York State:** Up to 10.9% marginal rate
  • **New York City:** Additional 3.876% if you live in NYC

    • A NYC resident hitting a $10,000 parlay could owe:

  • Federal (32% bracket): $3,200
  • NY State (6.85% bracket): $685
  • NYC (3.876%): $388
  • **Total tax:** ~$4,273 on a $10,000 win

    • **Yes, it's brutal.** Keep records of losses — you can deduct gambling losses against winnings on your federal return if you itemize.
