    Pennsylvania Gambling Taxes

    Online gambling winnings are fully taxable in Pennsylvania.

    Federal: Standard W-2G reporting applies. Winnings over $600 (slots) or $5,000 (table games) minus buy-in reported to IRS.

    Pennsylvania state: 3.07% tax on net gaming revenue (built into operator margins). Your actual income tax on gambling winnings follows normal state income tax rates (3.07%).

    Important: Keep detailed records of wins and losses. You can deduct losses up to wins on your federal return if itemizing.
