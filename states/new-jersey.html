Is Online Gambling Legal in New Jersey?

Yes — fully legal and heavily regulated.

New Jersey legalized online casino, poker, and sports betting in June 2018, making it the first state to do so. The New Jersey Division of Gaming Enforcement oversees all licensed operators with strict requirements.

Every licensed site is required to:

  • Verify your age and identity
  • Use geolocation to confirm you're in New Jersey

    New Jersey Gambling Taxes

    Online gambling winnings are taxable income in New Jersey.

    **Federal:** All gambling winnings are taxable. W-2G forms issued for winnings over $600 (lottery/slots) or $5,000 (table games/poker) minus buy-in.

    **New Jersey state tax rate:** 3.876% income tax rate applies to gambling winnings.

    **Casino/sports betting tax:** An additional 0.75% tax is withheld on sports betting winnings (collected by operators).

    **Important:** You can deduct gambling losses up to the amount of your winnings on your federal return if you itemize. Keep detailed records of all wins and losses.
