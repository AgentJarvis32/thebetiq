Is Online Gambling Legal in Colorado?

Yes — fully legal with no residency requirement.

Colorado was one of the first states to launch online sports betting (May 2020). The market has expanded rapidly with the addition of online casinos and poker-like games. All operators are licensed and regulated by the Colorado Division of Gaming.

    Colorado Gambling Taxes

    Online gambling winnings are taxable in Colorado.

    Federal: Standard W-2G reporting. Winnings over $600 (slots) or $5,000 (table games) minus buy-in reported to IRS.

    Colorado state: No state income tax on gambling winnings — this is a major advantage. You only owe federal tax on your winnings.

    Note: Operators must report large wins ($10,000+) for potential money laundering investigation compliance.
