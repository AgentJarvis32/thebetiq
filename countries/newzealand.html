NZ Gambling Law

The Gambling Act 2003 governs gambling in New Zealand. TAB NZ (formerly the Racing Board) holds the monopoly on sports and racing betting. Online casino games are technically prohibited for NZ-based operators, but international sites serving NZ players operate in a gray area.
