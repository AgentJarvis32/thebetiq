Australian Gambling Law

Sports betting in Australia is regulated at the state and territory level, with a national framework under the Interactive Gambling Act (IGA) 2001. All major operators hold ACMA-compliant licences (typically from the Northern Territory).

What's legal: Sports betting, racing (horses, greyhounds, harness), and fantasy sports.

What's NOT legal: Online casino games (slots, table games, live dealer), in-play betting on US sports (Australian sports are exempt), and online poker. The IGA specifically prohibits these for Australian-based operators serving Australian customers.

No Credit Betting

Australia banned credit card betting in 2021. All deposits must come from debit cards, bank transfer, POLi, or PayID. This was a harm minimization measure and is strictly enforced.
