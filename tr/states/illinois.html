    Illinois Gambling Taxes

    Online gambling winnings are taxable in Illinois.

    Federal: Standard W-2G reporting. Winnings over $600 (slots) or $5,000 (table games) minus buy-in reported to IRS.

    Illinois state: 4.95% flat income tax on all gambling winnings. Additionally, operators pay a 15% gross gaming tax on revenue (factored into promotions and odds).

    Tax responsibility: You must report all gambling income. Keep records of wins and losses. Losses can offset winnings on your return if itemizing.
