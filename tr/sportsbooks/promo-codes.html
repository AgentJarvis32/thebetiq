Understanding Bonus Bet Terms

Bonus bets pay winnings only. A $50 bonus bet winning at -110 pays $45 (not $95.45). The stake is not returned. For maximum value, use bonus bets at positive odds where the multiplier is bigger.

Expiration: Most bonus bets expire in 7-14 days. Claim only when you're ready to use them.

Minimum odds: Most books require bonus bets to be placed at -200 or better. Avoid betting -400 favorites with bonus bets.
