Welcome Bonus: Bet $5, Get $200

DraftKings' standard offer is one of the most straightforward in the market: bet $5 on any game and receive $200 in bonus bets regardless of whether your $5 bet wins or loses.

**The math:** $200 in bonus bets sounds great. Here's reality — bonus bets pay the winnings only, not the stake. If you bet $50 in bonus bets at -110 and win, you get $45.45 back (the winnings), not $95.45. Still valuable, but not quite $200 in real money.

**Best way to use bonus bets:** Spread them across moderate favorites (-130 or better) rather than parlays. You'll extract more value from the $200.

**Current offer may vary by state** — DraftKings adjusts their offers by location. Check their site for what's available in your state.

    Account Restrictions: The Honest Reality

    DraftKings, like all licensed US sportsbooks, limits winning accounts. If you consistently win — especially on player props or against the market — expect reduced limits or restricted markets on your account.

    This is an industry-wide practice, not unique to DraftKings. It's the most common complaint from sharp bettors.

    **If you bet recreationally:** You'll never encounter this. Limits are aimed at professional-level winning bettors.

    **If you're a serious bettor:** Offshore books (BetOnline, Bovada) rarely limit accounts. Keep a licensed book for the sign-up bonus and promos, and an offshore book as your primary for large bets.
